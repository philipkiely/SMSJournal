Features

Unlimited Journaling

Begin your SMSJournal with an untagged entry to see how the process works. If youâ€™d like to keep several different journals — a gratitude journal, a daily events journal, and a journal capturing adorable things your toddler says, for example — you can add a simple @tag, your message goes into a dedicated journal. Each message can have unlimited tags, and if a tag is used for the first time, SMSJournal automatically creates a new journal. An untagged message is posted in your default journal, named "SMSJournal."

Integrate Everywhere, Anytime

Add to your journals around the clock. SMSJournal works on anything that can send a text message, from a flip phone to a flagship smartphone. You can also set up Siri or Alexa to journal by voice.

Secure and Private

Our purpose is protecting your privacy. Every message is sent directly from your phone to Google Docs by an encrypted transmission through an AWS Lambda function. We do not read or log your messages, and the phone number, payment token, and encrypted journal names that we collect to provide the service are stored in a secure AWS database.
